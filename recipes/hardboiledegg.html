Hard Boiled Egg

Description

I know what you think: Hard Boiled Egg? Really? Well, sorry to bring it to you like that, but we've been doing it wrong all along. Here is the right way to do it.

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs (or more, or less)

Steps

  1. Place your eggs in a pot and pour in cold water until it covers all of the eggs.
  2. Place over high heat until water starts to simmer.
  3. Turn off the heat and cover the pot with a lid.
  4. Let it stand for 17 minutes. Don't cheat! No opening the lid.
  5. Pour out the hot water and pour cold water over the eggs. Redo one time.
  6. Let it stand until the eggs are cool (~ 20 minutes).
  7. Peel the eggs under running water.
  8. You did it! In less than an hour, you finally have your perfectly cooked hard boiled eggs. Taste the difference!
