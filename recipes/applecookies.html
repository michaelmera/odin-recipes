Apple Cookies

Description

These delicious Apple Cookies are perfect for an afternoon snack. Enjoy a strong sugar taste that will make you question your life choices.

Ingredients

  • Cookies
  • 4 Granny Smith Apples (cored and sliced)
  • White sugar (2 tablespoons)
  • Ground cinnamon (1 tablespoon)
  • Honey or maple syrup (Optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 120°C (250°F).
  2. Whisk sugar and cinnamon in a large bowl and add the apple slices. Toss to coat.
  3. Set the apple slices on baking sheets and bake in the preheated oven until browned (~2 hours).
  4. Let it cool and serve the apples on top of the cookies.
  5. (Optional) If you want to get diabetes you can add honey or maple syrup on top.
